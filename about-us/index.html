About Ananta Poly Plast

Your Trusted Partner in Plastic Packaging Solutions

Founded in 2020, Ananta Poly Plast has emerged as a premier manufacturer of high-quality PP corrugated sheets and boxes in Rajkot, Gujarat. Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility is equipped with cutting-edge technology to produce superior quality corrugated plastic products that meet international standards.

We take pride in our commitment to innovation, sustainability, and customer satisfaction. Our products are designed to provide optimal protection while being environmentally responsible, making them the preferred choice for businesses across various industries.
